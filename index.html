    The Founder

    Dr. Florence Temilade Akinwumiiju

    Dr. Florence Temilade Akinwumiiju is a passionate, dedicated and distinguished Doctor of Natural Health, often called the "Natural Therapy Doctor" in the U.S. She earned the highest honor in Doctor of Natural Health from Clinton College of Natural Health in Birmingham, Alabama. Since 2010, she has been helping people regain their health naturally, running multiple practice locations in the U.S., including one in Osteo, Nebutin.

    Her journey in healthcare is deeply rooted in faith and compassion. Alongside her work in natural health, she holds a Ph.D. in Christian Counseling with a specialization in addiction counseling. Over the years, she has cared for more than 4,000 patients, guided by the belief that We Care, God Heals.

    After spending over 4 decades in the U.S., Dr. Akinwumiiju made the decision to return to Nigeria, driven by a desire to help more people access quality natural treatments without the need to travel abroad. Her approach involves thorough health assessments that provide insights into the body's vital organs, helping to uncover the root causes of health challenges. She always emphasizes that she is not a healer—God is—but she is grateful to be used as a vessel to bring healing to those in need. Many of her patients, including those with serious conditions like cancer, have experienced remarkable recoveries.

    Beyond her medical practice, Dr. Akinwumiiju is also a writer and counselor. Her book, Addiction Christian Counseling, available on Amazon, offers valuable guidance for those struggling with addiction. She is also deeply involved in marriage counseling, helping couples navigate challenges and build stronger relationships.

    With a heart for service and a commitment to holistic healing, Dr. Akinwumiiju continues to touch lives, bringing hope and wellness to those who need it most.
